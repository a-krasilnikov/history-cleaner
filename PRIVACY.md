# Privacy Policy — History Auto-Cleaner

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

History Auto-Cleaner is a browser extension that automatically deletes
browser-history entries for websites you choose.

## What the extension accesses

- **Browsing history** — read locally to check each visited page against
  your rule list, and to delete matching entries. History is never
  transmitted anywhere; all processing happens on your device.
- **Your settings** (the list of sites and per-site options) — stored using
  `chrome.storage.sync` so they follow you across the Chrome browsers where
  you are signed in. This synchronization is performed by Chrome Sync (a
  Google service you control), not by us.

## What we collect

Nothing. This extension has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no
advertising. The developer does not receive, see, or store any of your data.
No data is ever sold or shared with third parties.

## Permissions

- `history` — to read and delete matching history entries.
- `storage` — to save your rule list and settings.
- `alarms` — to schedule the periodic background cleanup.

## Network

The extension makes no network requests of its own.

## Contact

For any questions, use the support channel on the extension's Chrome Web Store
listing.
